Introducing Sperling Supplies

We would like to introduce you to Sheila. She works for Sperling Supplies Corporation, which is a family owned supply store. Sperling Supplies offers computers, computer hardware, office supplies, etc. Over the last few years, Sperling Supplies has tripled its profits and doubled its personnel. As a result, Sheila has been hired to develop a new Human Resource system so that Sperling Supplies may offer benefits to its employees.

Over the course of this tutorial, you will see Sheila use Oracle SQL Developer to enable Sperling Supplies HR personnel to track employees, their dependents and job history. Within minutes after installing Oracle SQL Developer, Sheila will be able to create and manage database objects and build SQL queries without having to know the SQL syntax or build PL/SQL database objects to manipulate her data.

Throughout this tutorial, you will see demonstrations of the tasks you are to learn and have the opportunity to perform some of the tasks yourself.
